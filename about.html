The Observer

I am a street photographer based in Seoul, dedicated to capturing the fleeting, unscripted moments that define human existence in urban environments.

Armed with a single camera and a 35mm lens, I believe that the most profound stories are hidden in the mundane interactions of our daily lives. My work strips away distractions by focusing purely on form, light, and raw emotion, often utilizing stark black and white contrasts to emphasize the core narrative.

For me, photography is not just about taking pictures; it's a philosophy of observation.
